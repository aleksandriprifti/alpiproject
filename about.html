About

ALPi Flat Pack Assembly aims to provide the best services for all the kind of flat pack furniture that you have. The idea behind it is simple, we offer professional flatpack assembly service. From simple chest of drawers to wardrobe with sliding doors using our professional tools and knowledge. Our fitters will always try their best

We are familiar with all the major makes of flat packs and we can even assemble IKEA flat packs without any kind of instructions. We at ALPi consider SAFETY FIRST, together w'll ensure your home is a safe one. To keep you and your family safe and sound, we can secure the furniture to the wall.
